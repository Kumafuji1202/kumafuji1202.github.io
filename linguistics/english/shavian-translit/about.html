Purpose

I made this converter to check my own Shavian spelling from several years ago when I was really into the Shavian script. I was surprised to learn that the Shavian version of the top page of this site was full of typos and errors, so I think this tool will be helpful to both those who already know Shavian and those who don't.

The transliteration systems used

The last 6 letters are the extended Shavian letters. Not all fonts support them, so they might look identical to other letters in your environment.

Shavian

Neither of the transliteration systems are based on an actual codified standard.

The “orthodox‐like” system is meant to be readable by those who don't know how to read IPA.

The “conventional IPA‐like” system is meant to resemble the conventional diaphonemic IPA transcriptions of English words.

Abbreviations

Five basic word abbreviations to keep in mind:

  • 𐑞 (d͜h, ð) for the;
  • 𐑝 (v) for of;
  • 𐑯 (n) for and;
  • 𐑑 (t) for to (but not for two and too);
  • 𐑓 (f) for for;
𐑯𐑯𐑯 (nnn) stands for etc.

There are also a number of abbreviations listed and recommended at https://www.shavian.info/spelling/ that differ from standard Latin‐script spelling.

Punctuation

  • the middle dot · is placed before proper nouns when distinction is necessary.
